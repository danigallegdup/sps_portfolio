  • Sueno: Mental Health App: Innovation
  • Cognitive behavioral Therapy
  • Proactive: thoughts, actions, understanding emotions
  • Check it out: Sueno Prototype



Sueno is a mental health app that draws influence from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) by focusing on your emotions, thoughts, and actions. Sueno teaches and encourages you to create good quality thoughts and take proactive actions that improve your situation. It differs from other CBT resources because it focuses almost exclusively on the positive. There exists psychological evidence for why negative actions, thought, emotions seem to weigh heavier than all the good ones, called Negative Bias. Sueno fights your Negative Bias by keeping a log of all your wins and wise thoughts. Every human being of all ages will have to learn how to cope with a strong emotion, lucky Sueno has an emotional filter that can help in many ways.
